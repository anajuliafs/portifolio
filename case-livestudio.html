01 — Challenge

Two audiences,
one experience

The event platform had a growing need to support hybrid events — where some participants attend physically and others join remotely. The existing live streaming feature was bolted onto a system designed purely for in-person events, creating a fractured experience for both organizers and attendees.

Organizers struggled to manage both audiences simultaneously. Online attendees felt like second-class participants, with limited interaction tools and poor stream reliability feedback. The result was a high drop-off rate for remote participants during live sessions.

The core design challenge was to unify the experience — giving organizers a powerful but intuitive broadcast control panel, and giving remote attendees a sense of genuine presence and participation.

Key Insight

"Remote participants weren't just watching — they wanted to feel like they were actually there, not an afterthought."

02 — Process

From friction
to flow

Research
Contextual Inquiry
Shadowed 3 live events as an observer. Interviewed 6 event organizers and 12 remote attendees about their frustrations and workarounds.
Mapping
Dual Journey Maps
Created parallel journey maps for organizer and remote attendee, identifying moments of friction and opportunity for connection across both flows.
Design
Modular Studio UI
Designed a flexible live studio interface with separate modes for organizers and attendees, with real-time status, Q&A, reactions, and audience management tools.
Testing
Live Simulation
Ran 2 simulated live events with volunteer participants to test the full broadcast and attendance flows under realistic conditions before handoff.
03 — Solution

Features that
bridge worlds

📡
Unified Control Panel
A single broadcast dashboard for organizers to manage stream health, in-person AV, online attendees, Q&A moderation, and session timing — all without switching tools.
💬
Live Q&A & Reactions
Remote attendees can submit questions, react with emojis, and vote on questions — all surfaced to the organizer in a prioritized queue. In-person attendees see remote reactions on screens.
📊
Real-Time Analytics
Live view counts, engagement rates, drop-off points, and connection quality metrics — giving organizers actionable data during and after each session.
