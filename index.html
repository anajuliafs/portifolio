About

Designing with
purpose
and precision

I'm Ana Figueiredo, a UX Designer based in Jundiaí, Brazil. Before transitioning to design, I spent 12 years in public administration — managing contracts, overseeing government program accounting, and leading procurement processes at the Prefeitura Municipal de Jundiaí.

That experience gave me something most designers don't have: a deep understanding of complex systems, regulatory constraints, and the real weight of decisions made at scale. Combined with an early background in graphic design — where I handled client briefings, visual production, and budget management — I arrived at UX with roots in both structure and aesthetics.

Today I work at Accelera Technologies, focused on the energy market — designing SaaS products used by companies like EDP and Comgás. Working in a regulated, high-stakes sector means every decision needs to be grounded in real user needs and real business constraints. That's exactly where I want to be: at the intersection of complexity and clarity, designing with purpose.
